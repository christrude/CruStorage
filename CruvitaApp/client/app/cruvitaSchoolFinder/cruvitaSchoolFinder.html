The Cruvita School Finder

The Cruvita™ School Finder is designed to assist homebuyers when they need to find out what schools are associated with a specific address. The results returned by the School Finder are based upon school boundary information supplied by the U.S. Department of Education and/or 3rd party GIS vendors selected by Cruvita, LLC. Addresses are not verified by Cruvita, LLC. In order to verify that a specific address is zoned for a specific school you must contact that school, or the school district that school resides in.

The Cruvita™ School Finder is only placed on approved 3rd party sites. Cruvita, LLC is not responsible for the validity of the content of any 3rd party site. The Cruvita™ School Finder is placed on websites, or applications, that adhere to Cruvita, LLC’s Privacy Policy, Terms of Use and pay the appropriate subscription fees.
